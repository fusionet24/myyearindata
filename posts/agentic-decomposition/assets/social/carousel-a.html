3 Questions
Before You Agent

1
Does it need autonomous decision-making?
If it's deterministic, use a function.
2
Will it benefit from NL reasoning?
Agents shine with ambiguity.
3
Is coordination genuinely complex?
Multiple agents need multiple reasons.

"No" to all 3? Skip the agent.
